SuperModerno

SuperModerno is a platform dedicated to the history of technology, telling the stories of the people and companies that have helped shape the modern world.

Through an in-depth look at the innovations of the past, we offer keys to understanding the future.

About Us.

We share knowledge with the general public through online channels like YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn and beyond. We tell the lessons learned from history through public talks, corporate presentations and courses. Finally, we run innovation workshops where, starting from companies' own breakthroughs, we help develop the products and services of the future.

SuperModerno is created by Massimo Banzi, interaction designer, technologist and co-founder of Arduino, the computer that has allowed millions of people to innovate with digital technologies without being experts. A scholar of Innovation and the History of Technology, he translates the lessons of the past into maps for navigating the future.
